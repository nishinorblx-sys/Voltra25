VTR 25 / Voltra - Game Overview
================================

VTR 25, also called Voltra, is a Roblox football game built as a premium football
experience with a full menu hub, Ultimate Team-style progression, ranked modes,
and an active playable match prototype. The project is Rojo-ready and written in
Luau, with the server owning the important gameplay state and the client handling
presentation, prediction, input, and UI.


Current Core Gameplay
---------------------

- Playable football matches with server-owned ball physics, possession, goals,
  scores, stamina, AI, and set pieces.
- Player controls for movement, sprinting, shooting, passing, tackling, sliding,
  skill touches, close control, and player switching.
- Ball physics for shots, passes, lobs, clearances, corners, free kicks, and
  penalty kicks.
- Penalty system with slot-based aiming, goalkeeper guessing, goalkeeper dives,
  and post-goal ball physics.
- Goal detection using goal hitboxes/goal-line volumes.
- Post-goal ball behavior where scored shots keep their direction, continue
  physics, collide with the back net, rebound softly, and roll on the pitch.
- Net collision support for parts named Net, HomeNet, or AwayNet.
- Pitch grounding support for Pitch and PitchSurface so the ball rolls on the
  ground instead of phasing through.
- Goalkeepers that position on the line, predict shots, dive, save, catch, miss,
  and distribute the ball.
- AI teammates/opponents with passing, shooting, tackling, positioning, attacking
  runs, support options, and goalkeeper distribution.
- Match flow for kickoff, corners, goal kicks, throw-ins, free kicks, penalties,
  half-time, full-time, pauses, substitutions, and forfeits.
- Match stats and ratings including goals, shots, xG, passes, saves, tackles,
  corners, fouls, offsides, blocks, clearances, and match ratings.


Current Presentation Features
-----------------------------

- Broadcast-style camera with different set-piece and penalty camera behavior.
- Smooth predicted client ball visual with shot trails, flight markers, ball
  shadow, and rolling visuals.
- HUD with score, clock, stamina/endurance, charge bar, possession state, pause
  menus, substitutions, trainer prompts, and match messages.
- Minimap support with match-active toggles.
- Goal, half-time, stadium intro, and set-piece cutscene handling.
- Aim indicators, pass target markers, goal reticle, and free-kick/corner aiming
  helpers.
- Player indicators and name labels.
- R6 footballer animation support for idle, movement, sprinting, dribbling,
  passing, shooting, tackles, goalkeeper dives, saves, and celebrations.

- Full frontend hub framework with pages for Home, Play, Ranked, Ultimate Team,

- Settings for camera, controls, graphics/presentation preferences, trainer,

Important Technical Notes
-------------------------

- Server authority is used for possession, ball physics, scoring, stamina,
  set pieces, match stats, and profile/economy mutation.
- Client-side systems predict and smooth the ball visual, but do not own the
  true match result or ball authority.
- The project uses ReplicatedStorage/VTR/Shared for shared configs and types,
  ServerScriptService/VTRServer for authoritative services, and StarterPlayer
  scripts for client controllers/UI.
- The game can run in an empty Roblox place with generated fallback pitch/stadium
  elements, while also supporting existing stadium analysis.
- Current recent focus has been penalty behavior, scored-ball physics, net
  collision, PitchSurface grounding, and smooth post-goal visual movement.


Potential Features / Roadmap Ideas

- More realistic net physics with multiple net zones: soft catch, side netting,
  roof netting, and back-net rebound tuning.
- Better ball spin and curve after collisions, including side spin off posts and
  net deflections.
- Full post/woodwork collision behavior with realistic rebounds and sound effects.
- Penalty shootout mode with best-of-five rules, sudden death, keeper mind games,
  pressure UI, and cinematic replays.
- Replay system for goals, saves, fouls, and close offsides.
- VAR-style goal-line and offside presentation for dramatic moments.
- More free-kick tools: wall placement, run-up selection, knuckle shots, dipped
  shots, driven low shots, and custom set-piece routines.
- More goalkeeper animations: parries, low dives, one-handed saves, punches,
  catches, recoveries, and distribution throws.
- Stronger tactical AI with formations, pressing styles, defensive line height,
  build-up patterns, and player roles.
- Deeper player attributes affecting animations and match outcomes, such as
  composure, weak foot, curve, reactions, first touch, aggression, and balance.
- Career mode with seasons, transfers, training, injuries, contracts, and club
  progression.
- Ranked seasons with divisions, rewards, leaderboards, anti-cheat checks, and
  matchmaking polish.
- Live events, limited-time cards, Team of the Week, tournaments, and rotating
  objectives.
- Trading or transfer market systems for cards and cosmetics.
- More stadium customization: pitch patterns, weather, crowds, banners, goal
  nets, lighting, walkouts, chants, and camera packages.
- Pro Clubs-style custom player progression with build paths, perks, playstyles,
  cosmetics, and skill trees.
- Full mobile/controller control schemes.
- Training modes for shooting, penalties, free kicks, passing, dribbling,
  defending, and goalkeeper practice.
- Audio pass: crowd reactions, ball kicks, net hits, post hits, referee whistle,
  stadium ambience, menu music, and commentary stingers.
- Better onboarding/tutorial flow for new players.
- Analytics/debug tools for ball physics, AI decisions, possession, xG, and
  collision events.

   Goal, Net, PitchSurface, Pitch, HomeGoal, AwayGoal.
3. Add sound/visual feedback for net hits and goal rebounds.
4. Test the same scoring behavior in normal shots, free kicks, corners, and
   penalties so every goal path feels consistent.
5. Add a lightweight replay or slow-motion camera after penalties and big goals.
6. Continue improving goalkeeper saves and misses so they look intentional,
